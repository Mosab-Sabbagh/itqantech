Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our development process, technologies, and partnership models.

Need Personalized Assistance?

If you have specific questions about a project scope or require an in-depth consultation, our team is ready to help.

Connect with a Specialist

What is ITQAN's typical project methodology?

We primarily use an **Agile** methodology (Scrum/Kanban) to ensure maximum flexibility and transparency. This allows us to deliver working features quickly, incorporate feedback often, and adapt to changing requirements efficiently.

How long does it take to develop a standard website or app?

Timelines vary greatly based on scope. A simple marketing website may take 4-6 weeks, while a complex custom mobile application (MVP) typically requires 3 to 5 months. We provide detailed timelines after the initial scoping phase.

Can you work with my existing in-house technical team?

Absolutely. We are happy to integrate seamlessly with your existing teams, providing specialized expertise, filling capacity gaps, or handling specific project phases (e.g., UI/UX design or DevOps).

Which technologies do you specialize in?

Our core expertise includes modern web frameworks (React, Angular, Vue.js), backend systems (Node.js, Python/Django, PHP/Laravel), mobile development (Native iOS/Android or React Native/Flutter), and cloud platforms (AWS, Azure).

Do you provide post-launch maintenance and support?

Yes. We offer flexible maintenance and support contracts, including bug fixes, security updates, feature enhancements, and 24/7 monitoring, ensuring your product remains robust and up-to-date after launch.

Who owns the Intellectual Property (IP) of the code?

Upon final payment, the **client retains 100% ownership of the Intellectual Property (IP)** and source code for the developed solution. We ensure all contractual agreements clearly state this ownership transfer.

How is project cost determined?

Cost is based on the project's scope, complexity, required expertise, and delivery timeline. We use fixed-price models for well-defined scopes and time & material models for complex projects requiring high flexibility.

What is included in the "Digital Marketing" service?

Our marketing services cover comprehensive strategy, SEO, paid advertising (PPC), social media campaign management, content creation, and performance analytics to ensure measurable business growth.

Do you offer a warranty on the developed software?

Yes, we offer a warranty period (typically 30-90 days) post-launch to cover any functional bugs or issues that were not identified during the Quality Assurance phase. This ensures peace of mind after deployment.
